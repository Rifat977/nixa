Common questions
answered

At the heart of our commitment to delivering exceptional solutions lies transparency and trusted guidance.

  • What services do you offer?
    • Comprehensive visa application assistance.
    • Document preparation and review.
    • Tailored guidance for visa categories like student, work, and tourist visas.
    • University admission consulting and counseling.
  • What is the consultation process like?

    Our process is simple and client-focused:

    • Step 1: Initial consultation to understand your needs.
    • Step 2: Evaluation of eligibility and goals.
    • Step 3: Tailored recommendations and next steps.
  • How much do your services cost?

    Our initial consultation and basic services are completely free! Additional services, if required, will be discussed transparently.

  • How do I get started with your services?
    • Reach out to us via phone, email, or by visiting one of our branches.
    • Share your goals, and we’ll guide you through the next steps.
  • What is your success rate with visa applications?

    We are proud of our strong track record, with a 100% success rate for Malaysia. Our meticulous planning ensures smooth processing.

  • What documents are required for the visa application?
    • A valid passport (minimum validity of 18 months).
    • Malaysian-sized photos.
    • Last academic transcript and certificate.
    • Curriculum Vitae (CV).
    • English proficiency certificate or equivalent.
    • Research proposal (for research-based programs).
  • Can you help with university admissions?

    Yes, we specialize in assisting students with university admissions, offering end-to-end support.

  • How long does the visa process take?

    Processing times vary based on the visa category and country. We provide accurate timelines during consultation.

  • What if my visa application is rejected?

    We analyze rejection reasons and provide guidance on reapplying or exploring alternatives.

  • Do you offer post-arrival services?

    Yes, we provide airport pick-up, accommodation arrangements, and more for a smooth transition.

  • Can I track my visa application status?

    Yes, we provide regular updates and keep you informed about your application progress.
