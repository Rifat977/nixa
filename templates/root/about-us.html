About Nixa Global
Your Partner for Global Success

Nixa Global Sdn. Bhd., incorporated on March 3, 2023, in Malaysia (Registration No. 1501679K / 202301007758), is a versatile consultancy firm dedicated to connecting individuals and businesses with global opportunities. With headquarters in Malaysia and a fully operational branch in Dhaka, Bangladesh, we deliver comprehensive services across education, immigration, travel, and business sectors.

Our Services

  • Educational Consultancy: Helping students gain admission to top universities and guiding them through the visa process.
  • Visa Assistance: Offering end-to-end support for student, tourist, and second home visas, as well as other visa categories.
  • Travel Services: Facilitating air ticket booking, customized tour packages, and tourism solutions for individuals and families.
  • Post-Arrival Support: Assisting with accommodation, health insurance, and orientation to ensure a seamless transition abroad.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between aspirations and opportunities by delivering reliable, innovative, and customer-focused solutions. We empower individuals and businesses through seamless access to global education, visa services, travel, and construction expertise, ensuring success at every step.

Vision

Our vision is to become a globally recognized leader in education consultancy, visa facilitation, and business services. We inspire trust, drive innovation, and create pathways for individuals and organizations to achieve their goals while fostering excellence and sustainability.

Our Immigration Service History

At Nixa Global Sdn. Bhd., we take pride in transforming immigration challenges into success stories. Since our establishment in 2023, we have been dedicated to providing seamless immigration solutions tailored to the needs of students, professionals, and families. With a 100% success rate in Malaysian visa applications, our services are backed by meticulous planning, expert advice, and a commitment to excellence.

  • Student Visas: Connecting students with top universities and simplifying visa processes.
  • Second Home Visas: Helping individuals and families with programs like Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H).
  • Tourist & Work Visas: Enabling travel enthusiasts and professionals to explore global opportunities with ease.
